The Headache of SFTP Compliance

Amadeus is requiring agencies to switch to SFTP to download critical files like AIR records. For small and medium agencies, this is a major challenge. You're now faced with technical hurdles you shouldn't have to worry about:

Security Risks

Setting up a secure server is complex. A small mistake can expose your business and client data to threats.

High Costs & Maintenance

Hiring an IT consultant or using expensive software is costly. Plus, servers need constant monitoring and updates.

Workflow Disruption

Your team needs files on their local machines to work with your back-office systems. How do you bridge the gap from a cloud server to your local network securely and reliably?

A "Set It and Forget It" Solution

SapeBridge is a simple, secure service built to replace the Amadeus Stand-Alone Print Emulator (SAPE). We handle all the technical complexity of SFTP so you can keep your familiar workflow.

How It Works:

  1. 1. We give you secure SFTP credentials for Amadeus.
  2. 2. Amadeus drops files into our secure system.
  3. 3. Our lightweight Windows client automatically downloads the files to your designated local folder.
  4. 4. Your back-office system processes the files just like it always has.
